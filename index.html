Ashok Kuikel

Ashok Kuikel speaks and writes about the future of education and works with a portfolio of education organizations to improve the life of each and every student.

He is the author of CHOOSING COLLEGE, which strips away the noise around college to help students and parents understand why they are going to school and how to make better choices, as well as aids schools in designing better experiences.
